# **App Name**: Saheli Knee Care

## Core Features:

- Symptom Analyzer: AI tool that identifies potential causes and provides personalized advice based on reported symptoms, dietary habits (veg/non-veg), and lifestyle factors specific to older Indian women.
- Dietary Recommendations: Provides customized dietary plans for managing knee pain, with options for both vegetarian and non-vegetarian preferences, highlighting nutrient-rich Indian foods that support joint health.
- Exercise Guides: Step-by-step visual guides for low-impact exercises tailored for older Indian women, designed to improve knee strength and flexibility while accommodating cultural clothing and home environments.
- Home Remedy Database: A curated collection of traditional Indian home remedies for knee pain, vetted for safety and efficacy, with user reviews and ratings to build community trust.
- Doctor Locator: Locates nearby doctors and specialists experienced in treating knee pain in older adults, with a focus on culturally sensitive care.
- Community Forum: A moderated forum where women can share experiences, ask questions, and support each other in managing knee pain.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm orange (#FFA500) to evoke comfort and well-being, inspired by the warmth of Indian spices and culture.
- Background color: Soft beige (#F5F5DC) for a calming and supportive feel, providing a gentle backdrop for the content.
- Accent color: Muted green (#8FBC8F) to represent health and natural remedies, complementing the orange and beige tones.
- Body font: 'Literata' serif for readability and a connection to traditional knowledge.
- Headline font: 'PT Sans' sans-serif paired with 'Literata' for headlines, to ensure a modern, friendly feel while maintaining readability.
- Use clear and culturally relevant icons. Example: silhouettes of women performing gentle yoga poses or common Indian spices known for anti-inflammatory properties.
- Ensure a clean, uncluttered layout with clear hierarchies. Prioritize legibility with large, adjustable font sizes and high contrast.
- Subtle transitions and micro-interactions to provide feedback and guidance, especially within the exercise guides, using a speed that respects an older audience.
